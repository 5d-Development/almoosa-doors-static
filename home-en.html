OUR STORY

Our story began with the first door in the early 1980s when we wanted to change the gate of our house to a smart gate that opens with a remote control. However, we couldn’t find a company that offered such a gate in Saudi Arabia. This is where the idea came from.

After ordering and installing the first door, we found an opportunity and a need in the local market for similar products. This helped us grow and evolve by constantly searching for the best solutions and innovations. From an idea and ambition, it turned into a tangible reality.

WHAT WE OFFER

We have been operating in this field since 1984. We take pride in being one of the pioneers in the industry and owning the largest door manufacturing factory in Saudi Arabia.

With extensive expertise in manufacturing and supplying high-quality doors, we aim to grow in the local market and reach the global market in alignment with the vision of Saudi Arabia 2030.

ABOUT OUR PRODUCTS

Our products are meticulously designed and carefully crafted to meet the highest standards of quality and functionality. We offer a wide range of innovative and reliable products that cater to various industries and needs.
