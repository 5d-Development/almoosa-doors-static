ABOUT US

OUR HISTORY

Our story began with the first door in the early 1980s when we wanted to change the gate of our house to a smart gate that opens with a remote control. However, we couldn't find a company that offered such a gate in Saudi Arabia. This is where the idea came from. After ordering and installing the first door, we found an opportunity and a need in the local market for similar products. This helped us grow and evolve by constantly searching for the best solutions and innovations. From an idea and ambition, it turned into a tangible reality.

OUR MISSION

Our mission at Al-Moosa Doors Company is to offer a wide range of high-quality products, including garage doors, arms, windows, shop doors, and warehouse doors, as well as bulletproof and fire-resistant doors. We strive to achieve customer satisfaction by providing products manufactured in Saudi Arabia and locally and globally accredited. Our vision is to prioritize quality and deliver fast and efficient service to ensure customer satisfaction and trust.

OUR VISION

Our vision at Al-Mousa Doors Company is to become a leader in the industry of high-quality doors and products, and to be the preferred choice for customers in Saudi Arabia and globally. We aim to expand our operations, offer innovative and sustainable solutions, and build strong partnerships with our clients for sustainable growth and development.

OUR GOALS

  • To consistently deliver high-quality and innovative door products to meet the diverse needs of our customers.
  • To expand our market presence and reach new customers both locally and internationally.
  • To enhance customer satisfaction through excellent service, timely delivery, and competitive pricing.
  • To continuously improve our manufacturing processes and technology to ensure product excellence and efficiency.
  • To foster a culture of teamwork, professionalism, and continuous learning among our employees for personal and organizational growth.
